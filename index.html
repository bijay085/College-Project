What is FraudShield?

FraudShield is a real-time fraud-detection engine that uses explainable logic and behavioral intelligence to spot risky transactions before they hit your books.

Why Choose FraudShield?

  • Instant scoring: get a clear risk score in milliseconds
  • Explainable rules: know exactly why a transaction flagged
  • Behavioral checks: detect bots, stolen cards, disposable emails
